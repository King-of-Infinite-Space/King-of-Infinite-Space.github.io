# For Stephen

He's gone
leaving his wheeled throne
Unbounded by the imperfect shell
wandering in infinite space

He's gone
beyond the event horizon
But you'll feel the radiation
engraved with his name

2018.3.14 MCR
RIP Stephen Hawking
